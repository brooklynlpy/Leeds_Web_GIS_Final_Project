Carbendazim Concentration Web GIS

Interactive visualisation of carbendazim concentrations across environmental media in China.

Background

Carbendazim is a widely used benzimidazole fungicide that has been detected in multiple environmental media, including soil and water, and has attracted attention because of its ecological risks and potential exposure pathways.

This project presents an interactive Web GIS of carbendazim occurrence in China. Users can explore spatial patterns, filter results by media, year and month, and review supporting study information linked to individual sampling locations.

Chemical structure

Carbendazim, molecular formula C9H9N3O2.
